Project Overview

The King Fahd Land Cleanup project was a comprehensive environmental restoration initiative targeting a 500 square meter abandoned government land in the heart of Riyadh. This area had been neglected for several years, accumulating various types of waste and debris.

What We Accomplished

  • Removed 2.5 tons of mixed waste and debris
  • Sorted and recycled 80% of collected materials
  • Cleared vegetation overgrowth and invasive plants
  • Prepared land for future development projects
  • Generated revenue through recycling operations

Environmental Impact

This project prevented 2.5 tons of waste from potentially contaminating the surrounding environment. The recycling efforts contributed to the circular economy while the land restoration improved the aesthetic and environmental quality of the King Fahd Street area.
