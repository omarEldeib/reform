Project Overview

The Palm District Land Reform project focused on restoring a coastal area in Jeddah that had been affected by urban waste accumulation. This 750 square meter site required specialized cleanup techniques due to its proximity to the Red Sea coast.

What We Accomplished

  • Coastal waste removal and beach restoration
  • Palm tree area rehabilitation
  • Marine-safe waste disposal practices
  • Soil treatment and replanting preparation
  • Community engagement and education

Environmental Impact

This coastal restoration project protected the Red Sea marine ecosystem from land-based pollution. The cleanup prevented harmful materials from entering the ocean while restoring the natural beauty of Jeddah's coastline.
