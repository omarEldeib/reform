Project Overview

The Eastern Dammam Land Development project was our most comprehensive cleanup initiative, covering 1200 square meters of former industrial land. This site required specialized handling due to industrial contamination and heavy machinery removal.

This industrial land restoration project removed significant environmental hazards from the Dammam area. The comprehensive cleanup and soil treatment ensured the land meets safety standards for future development while maximizing material recovery and recycling.
